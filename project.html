Nykaa Financial Performance Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive analysis of Nykaa's (FSN E-Commerce Ventures) financial performance, covering multiple time horizons (yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, nine-monthly). It aims to provide insights into Nykaa's key financial metrics, including revenue, profitability, expenses, cash flow, and financial ratios, to help users understand the company's financial health and make informed decisions.

Technical Details

  • Data Sources: Financial data was extracted from MoneyControl using Python and the pandas library, then cleaned and processed for analysis of PDF format containing Consolidated financial reports, yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, and nine-monthly data for key financial statements (Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss, Cash Flow, and Financial Ratios).
  • Data Transformation: Power Query was used to clean, transform, and prepare the data for analysis.
  • Data Modeling: A star schema data model was created in Power BI Desktop, with relationships established between the fact table (cleaned_yearly) and various dimension tables.

    • Star schema data model for Nykaa Financial Performance Dashboard.

  • DAX Measures: Numerous DAX measures were created to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs), ratios, and trends.
  • Report Design: The Z-pattern layout was used to structure the reports, guiding user attention and creating a clear visual hierarchy.

Visualizations

A variety of visualizations were used to effectively communicate insights:

  • Cards: Used to display key KPIs like revenue, profit, and EPS.
  • Gauge Charts: Used to visualize profit margin, debt-to-equity ratio, and other key ratios.
  • Line Charts: Used to show trends in revenue, expenses, and ratios over time.
  • Column Charts: Used to compare performance across different periods or categories.
  • Stacked Column Charts: Used to visualize the composition of revenue and expenses.
  • Waterfall Charts: Used to illustrate cash flow movements.
  • Treemaps: Used to show the breakdown of assets.
  • Pie Charts: Used to display the composition of liabilities.
  • Scatter Plots: Used to explore the relationship between different financial ratios.
  • Tables: Used to display detailed financial data.
  • Slicers: Used to enable interactive filtering of data by year, quarter, etc.
  • Tooltips: Advanced tooltips were implemented, including report page tooltips and conditional formatting, to provide more context and insights.

Based on the Nykaa Yearly Report, here are some key conclusions and insights you can include in your report summary:

Revenue Growth

  • Nykaa has demonstrated strong revenue growth over the past few years.
  • "Net Sales/Income from operations" has increased from Rs. 1,765.33 crore in Mar '20 to Rs. 6,385.63 crore in Mar '24.

Profitability

  • The company's profitability has also shown improvement, with "Net Profit/(Loss) For the Period" increasing from a loss of Rs. 16.34 crore in Mar '20 to a profit of Rs. 43.72 crore in Mar '24.
  • The "Basic EPS" has also increased significantly, from -0.39 in Mar '20 to 0.11 in Mar '24.

Key Expenses

  • The major expenses for Nykaa include "Consumption of Raw Materials," "Purchase of Traded Goods," and "Employees Cost."
  • While these expenses have increased in absolute terms, they have generally remained stable or decreased as a percentage of revenue, indicating efficient expense management.

Other Observations

  • Nykaa has consistently generated "Other Income," which contributes to its overall profitability.
  • The company has utilized both borrowings and equity for its financing needs.
  • The "Net P/L After M.I & Associates" has also shown a positive trend, increasing from a loss of Rs. 16.61 crore in Mar '20 to a profit of Rs. 32.27 crore in Mar '24.

These conclusions highlight Nykaa's strong financial performance, marked by consistent revenue growth, improving profitability, and efficient expense management.

Based on the Financial Ratio Report, here are some key conclusions and insights you can include in your report summary:

Profitability Ratios

  • Nykaa's profitability ratios have shown improvement over the years, with "Operating Profit Margin(%)" increasing from 4.58 in Mar'20 to 5.42 in Mar'24.
  • The company's "Net Profit Margin(%)" has also shown a positive trend, increasing from -0.93 in Mar'20 to 0.50 in Mar'24.
  • "Return On Net Worth(%)" has increased from -5.33 in Mar'20 to 2.55 in Mar'24, indicating better utilization of shareholder's funds.

Liquidity and Solvency Ratios

  • Nykaa's "Current Ratio" has improved from 0.83 in Mar'20 to 0.95 in Mar'24, indicating better short-term liquidity.
  • The company's "Quick Ratio" has also shown a positive trend, increasing from 0.74 in Mar'20 to 0.84 in Mar'24.
  • "Debt Equity Ratio" has decreased from 0.86 in Mar'20 to 0.54 in Mar'24, indicating lower reliance on debt financing.

Management Efficiency Ratios

  • Nykaa's "Inventory Turnover Ratio" has improved from 3.97 in Mar'20 to 5.36 in Mar'24, indicating better inventory management.
  • The company's "Debtors Turnover Ratio" has increased from 22.61 in Mar'20 to 31.52 in Mar'24, indicating efficient collection of receivables.

These conclusions highlight Nykaa's strong financial performance, marked by improving profitability, better liquidity, lower debt reliance, and efficient management of inventory and receivables.
