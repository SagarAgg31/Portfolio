Real Estate Sales Performance Report

Introduction

  • Project Goal:To track and analyze the sales performance of real estate teams and individual agents, providing insights into lead generation, conversion rates, sales trends, and overall team productivity.
  • Target Audience:Real estate team leaders and sales managers. The report may also be useful for individual agents, brokers, and other stakeholders in the real estate business.

Empathy Map

    Pains

  • Lack of visibility into individual agent performance.
  • Difficulty identifying areas for improvement.
  • Time-consuming manual reporting.
  • Inconsistent data across different systems.
  • Keeping up with agent needs and concerns.

    Gains

  • Increased team productivity and sales.
  • Improved agent morale and retention.
  • Data-driven insights for better decision-making.
  • Efficient performance tracking and reporting.
  • Streamlined communication and collaboration.

Technical Details

  • Data Sources:The data has been generated from ChatGPT
  • Data Transformation: Power Query was used to clean, transform, and prepare the data for analysis.
  • Data Modeling: A star schema data model was created in Power BI Desktop, with relationships established between the fact table (sales) and various dimension tables.
  • DAX Measures: Numerous DAX measures were created to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs), ratios, and trends.
  • Report Design: The Z-pattern layout was used to structure the reports, guiding user attention and creating a clear visual hierarchy.

Visualizations

A variety of visualizations were used to effectively communicate insights:

  • Cards: To display key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total sales, lead conversion rate, and top-performing agents.
  • Gauge Charts: To visualize sales trends over time, track lead generation, and monitor changes in average selling prices.
  • Line Charts: To compare the performance of different agents or teams, analyze sales by property type, or visualize lead conversion rates by source.
  • Column Charts: Used to compare performance across different periods or categories.
  • Clustered Column Charts: For comparing the performance of different restaurants, analyzing day-by-day comparisons of key metrics, and understanding customer satisfaction across segments.
  • Funnel Charts: To visualize the lead conversion process and identify potential bottlenecks.
  • Tables: To provide detailed information about sales transactions, agent performance, or lead status, allowing for sorting and filtering.
  • Tooltips: Advanced tooltips were implemented, including report page tooltips and conditional formatting, to provide more context and insights.

Key Performance Indicators

  • Lead Conversion Rate: 20.37% (Slightly above the overall average, indicating efficiency in converting leads to sales).

Sales Volume Comparison

  • Team B leads in sales volume, followed by Team E and Team C. This suggests that Team B has effective sales strategies and/or a strong client base.

Lead Conversion Rate and Deal Size by Lead Source

  • Email Campaign: While Team A has the highest lead conversion rate (22.01%) from email campaigns, Team B generates the highest sales volume ($136,664,090) from this source. This could indicate that Team B attracts higher-value clients through email campaigns.
  • Open House: Team C excels in lead conversion (22.25%) from open houses. However, Team B again leads in sales volume ($136,664,903), suggesting they effectively leverage open houses to close deals.
  • Referral: Team A has the highest conversion rate for referrals (21.75%), but Team B maintains the highest sales volume ($136,664,090).

Overall, Team B demonstrates strong performance across various metrics. Their ability to generate high sales volumes, despite not always having the highest lead conversion rates, suggests they are successful in securing and closing larger deals.

Actionable Insights

  • Share best practices from Team B with other teams to improve their performance.
  • Continue monitoring Team B's performance to identify areas for further improvement and maintain their strong results.
