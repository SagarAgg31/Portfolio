Online Food Delivery Dashboard

Introduction

  • Project Goal:To provide a data-driven overview of the online food delivery service's performance, focusing on key metrics such as order volume, delivery efficiency, customer demographics, popular cuisines, and overall business growth.
  • Target Audience:Primarily the Operational Manager of the online food delivery service. The dashboard will also be valuable for other stakeholders, including restaurant partners, delivery partners, and the marketing team.

Empathy Map

Technical Details

  • Data Sources:The data has been generated from ChatGPT
  • Data Transformation: Power Query was used to clean, transform, and prepare the data for analysis.
  • Data Modeling: A star schema data model was created in Power BI Desktop, with relationships established between the fact table (orders) and various dimension tables.
  • DAX Measures: Numerous DAX measures were created to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs), ratios, and trends.
  • Report Design: The Z-pattern layout was used to structure the reports, guiding user attention and creating a clear visual hierarchy.

Visualizations

A variety of visualizations were used to effectively communicate insights:

  • Cards: Used to display key performance indicators (KPIs) like average delivery time, order completion rate, and customer satisfaction score.
  • Gauge Charts: To visualize the on-time delivery rate and week-over-week changes in delivery times against target ranges.
  • Line Charts: To show trends in order volume, average delivery time, complaint rates, and customer satisfaction over time.
  • Column Charts: Used to compare performance across different periods or categories.
  • Clustered Column Charts: For comparing the performance of different restaurants, analyzing day-by-day comparisons of key metrics, and understanding customer satisfaction across segments.
  • Bar Charts: To display the average customer rating per restaurant.
  • Tables: To provide detailed information about orders, restaurants, customer feedback, and breakdowns of complaints.
  • Tooltips: Advanced tooltips were implemented, including report page tooltips and conditional formatting, to provide more context and insights.

Actionable Insights

  • Investigate the order completion rate drop: Analyze order cancellations, restaurant performance, and delivery partner data to pinpoint the cause.
  • Analyze delivery partner performance: Gather more detailed data on individual delivery partners for both days to identify any performance trends or areas for improvement.
  • Monitor customer feedback: Pay close attention to customer reviews and feedback to understand any emerging issues or trends.
  • Continue to track key metrics: Continue monitoring these metrics daily to identify patterns and make data-driven adjustments to improve overall efficiency and customer satisfaction.

While we saw an impressive 84.32% increase in weekly revenue compared to the previous week, the high customer churn rate of 44.23% remains a significant concern. Acquiring 30 new customers this week is positive, but we need to focus on improving customer retention. Analysis reveals a correlation between order volume, average delivery time, and complaint rate, with all three peaking mid-week. This suggests that operational efficiency might be strained during peak order periods, leading to longer delivery times and increased complaints. Strategies to address these issues could include optimizing delivery routes, improving order fulfillment processes during peak hours, and proactively addressing customer concerns to enhance satisfaction and loyalty."

Actionable Insights:

  • Investigate Churn: Conduct thorough analysis to understand why customers are churning. Gather feedback, analyze customer segments, and identify areas for improvement in the customer experience.
  • Optimize Delivery Operations: Focus on improving delivery efficiency, especially during peak mid-week periods. This could involve optimizing delivery routes, improving dispatching processes, or incentivizing delivery partners for faster deliveries.
  • Enhance Customer Service: Develop strategies to proactively address customer complaints and improve overall satisfaction. This might include faster response times, more effective complaint resolution, or loyalty programs to encourage repeat business.
  • Review Marketing Strategies: Evaluate your marketing campaigns to ensure they are effectively attracting and retaining customers. Consider targeted promotions or loyalty programs to incentivize repeat business.
